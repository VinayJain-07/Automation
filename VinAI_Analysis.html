The Calculation Logic
DM1
Top-Down Macro: India's national digital ad market is ~₹44,800 Cr in 2026. South India holds ~34.6% of that. Hyderabad captures ~30% of South India's footprint driven by IT exports (₹3.13 lakh Cr) and GCC density → yields ₹4,700 Cr from above.
DM2
Bottom-Up Cross-Check: Aggregating sector-level spend across the four verticals gives ₹4,600 Cr — within 2.2% of the top-down figure. This bi-directional convergence is why the range is reported as ₹4,600–4,800 Cr.
AI1
Global Benchmark: The global AI automation market crossed $169 Billion in 2026. India commands ~8.5% of that ($14.4B). Hyderabad captures 15–20% of India's tech footprint → $1.75B (~₹16,750 Cr) for the city's total addressable AI automation market.
AI2
Marketing Subset: Autonomous ad bidding, dynamic lead scoring, and agentic SEO account for ~10% of the AI automation stack → ₹1,675 Cr, or ~$175M. This is the specific opportunity for agencies willing to cross the bridge.
